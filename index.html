What we do

Underfloor Heating Installation

We offer complete bespoke underfloor heating solutions for both commercial and residential projects across London and Home Counties.

Our specialty lies within the design, supply, and installation of bespoke systems to meet client needs, whether wet or electric underfloor systems.

Contact us now to discover how our team can advise and design the perfect system for you.

Whether you want a current system refurbishing or upgrading, or even a new build project; we cater to all client requests. We provide a consistent and professional service from the initial project stage through to completion.

We cover all projects from self-build, residential and commercial new build, and refurbished properties. With our industry-leading warranty, you can rest assured that your heated floors will perform great for decades to come.

Underfloor Heating Repairs

Thermaze offers a repair service to customers who are having Wet or Electric underfloor heating problems or have had a bad experience and the installer has washed their hands of it and won't come back.

We will come to your property, take an impartial look at the system and listen to your experiences to get the system back up to full working speed in no time!

The most common problems we find are wiring issues, zones not heating up, air locked pipes, worn out pumps and these are all issues that can be easily sorted out.

One phrase we hear very often is “but what if the problem is under the floor?” and we want to reassure you that in 99% of cases the problem with your underfloor heating system is normally above ground!
